about me

Hello, I am Julia Child. I gained fame with my culinary show The French Chef and my cookbook Mastering the Art of French Cooking. I also founded The Julia Child Foundation for Gastronomy and Culinary Arts. I received several Emmys and the Peabody Award for my work, and had a rose named after me.
